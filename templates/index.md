# Template Index

| name | file | best for | position |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| hook-stat | hook-stat.md | Bold statistic that reframes the topic | Slide 1 |
| step-by-step | step-by-step.md | Numbered technique or process | Middle slides |
| comparison | comparison.md | Side-by-side contrast of two approaches | Middle slides |
| listicle | listicle.md | Ranked tips or teaser hook | Slide 1 or middle |

## Selection Rules
- Slide 1: use `hook-stat` if research contains a specific statistic; otherwise use `listicle` in teaser mode
- Middle slides: process/sequence → step-by-step | contrast/alternatives → comparison | discrete tips → listicle
- Final slide: copy-only CTA, no template
- Use max 2 different templates per carousel for visual consistency
